Tópicos:
- extrair objetos de imagens
- paste image to another, alpha channel
- blend two different images through interpolations at different levels of Laplacian pyramids. [Burt and Adelson 1983]
- Poisson image editing, on the other hand, blends two images through Poisson equations. [Perez et al. 2003]

GrabCut [Rother et al. 2004] and Lazy Snapping [Li et al.
2004] can be used to produce Wobj once W0 is given.
